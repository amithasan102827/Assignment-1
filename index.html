Baked Pesto Salmon

Mix together store-bought pesto and dijon mustard (about 50/50) in a bowl and spread generously over salmon filets. Bake on 350 degrees for 15 minutes (a little more if you like it more well done).

Chicken Stir Fry

Slice 2 chicken breasts into strips or cubes. Brown the chicken in a pan and set aside. Chop 2 heads of broccoli, 2 zucchinis, 1 small piece of ginger and 1 garlic clove.

Broccoli Rabe Pasta

Saute 2 bunches of broccoli rabe with olive oil and garlic. Meanwhile, cook 1 pack of spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Once spaghetti in cooked, mix it into the pan with the broccoli and garlic.
